Welcome! Use this website to see how San Antonio's lots are defined and valued by the city.

Use the dropdown in the top right-hand corner of the map to sort San Antonio's inner-loop lots by their defined use.

Click any lot to see its use, the value the city has appraised it at, the year it was built, and the name of the business or organization (if applicable).

The data is courtesy of the Bexar County Apprasial District. Any "null" values you get after clicking on a lot either means that the county does not have this data, or that information is not applicable to the selected lot.
